    Per D-V3-Phase6-C LOCKED: 1 channel `settings:invalidate` cho ca 3 category

    #: category settings invalidate. Subscriber pattern `redis.pubsub().subscribe(channel)`.
    SETTINGS_INVALIDATE_CHANNEL: str = "settings:invalidate"
